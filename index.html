🎮 How to Play

Your mission as a skilled collector is to roam the forest and gather magical ingredients for your potions.

  • 🍀 Collect the ingredients that appear throughout the forest.
    Each item you collect gives you +10 points to your score.
  • 💔 But be careful! Avoid the bugs or you'll lose lives.
  • 🌟 Catch the mysterious fruit to become immune and move faster
    for a few seconds. Plus, you'll earn +30 points!
  • ​🐝 If the bugs hit you three times… it's Game Over!
  • 🔁 But don't worry, you can always try again!
